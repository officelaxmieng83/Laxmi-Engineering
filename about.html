About Laxmi Engineering

Delivering world-class heavy-duty bandsaw industrial solutions since 1983.

M/s. Laxmi Engineering is a globally recognized pioneer in manufacturing Heavy-Duty Horizontal and Vertical Band Saw Machines. Headquartered in West Bengal, India, we have spent over four decades engineering high-precision machinery, custom spare parts, and dependable maintenance services for heavy industries worldwide.

The Laxmi Engineering Advantage

  • 40+ Years of Expertise: Established in 1983, we bring deep technical knowledge and proven engineering reliability to every project.
  • Comprehensive Portfolio: We specialize in both heavy-duty Horizontal and Vertical Bandsaw machines tailored to diverse industrial needs.
  • End-to-End Support: We manufacture genuine spare parts and offer expert servicing to ensure your operations never stop.
  • Global Export Footprint: Our high-standard machinery is trusted and exported to clients across the world.

Our Core Offerings

  • Heavy-Duty Bandsaw Machines: Precision-engineered Horizontal and Vertical Bandsaw machines built for tough cutting applications.
  • Genuine Spare Parts: High-grade, durable spare parts designed to maximize machine lifespan and efficiency.
  • Maintenance & Servicing: Skilled overhaul support to optimize machine performance.
